About Us

Your travel companion

since 1997

AJKV Tours & Travels was founded in 1997 by the travel enthusiast couple, Mr. Upendra and Mrs. C. Nirmala , and is synonymous throughout India for coach tours and customized tours, all over India. AJKV Tours & Travels is recognized by the Government of India and Karnataka, Dept. of Tourism and accepted by all Nationalised and Non-nationalised Institutions for availing LTC/LTF Facility.

Our network of branches & agents are spread all over Karnataka & neighbouring States. We own a fleet of Twenty luxury coaches. AJKV Tours & Travels specializes in handling special groups, leisure groups, Honey-Moon Packages, family groups, School groups and caters to any customized or tailor made programmes.

With the vision “To conquer the travel world by spreading its wings of quality service” AJKV Tours & Travels strives hard to make it possible for its customers to visit any corner of the world through its quality service in the field of tourism.

The objectives of AJKV Tours & Travels are as follows:

  1. To continue to be the market leader in domestic tours in Southern India.
  2. To provide quality service to the customers.
  3. To ensure safety and security of the customers in the course of their travel with us.
  4. To ensure that our customers get value for their money and meet their expectations.
  5. To constantly improve the organizational processes of rendering service to the customers.
  6. To adhere to legal and other regulatory requirements of the organization.
  7. To create a good working environment for the employees and enable them to continuously improve their performance levels.
